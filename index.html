About BuddyPay

BuddyPay is the financial support vertical of Studiencollege, built to simplify the most complex part of studying abroad — managing finances.

We work with trusted lenders, financial institutions, and education partners to ensure students meet university and visa requirements with confidence.

Our Services

Student Loans

Comprehensive Loan Support: We help you access education loans tailored for international study — including term loans, repayment options, moratorium periods, and flexible interest structures.

Partnered Lenders: Our vetted lending partners provide competitive rates and transparent terms so you can plan with confidence.

Quick Eligibility Checks: Pre-qualify with minimal documentation. We guide you step-by-step from application to disbursement.

Show Money

Visa-Compliant Financial Proof: Show Money (Proof of Funds) satisfies embassy financial requirements by demonstrating you have the necessary funds for tuition and living expenses.

Document Preparation: We assist with bank statements, affidavits, and formal letters formatted to embassy expectations.

Verification Support: Guidance through documentation checks ensures smoother visa interviews and approvals.

Scholarships

Curated Scholarship Matching: We scan local and international scholarship databases to find awards you actually qualify for.

Application Preparation: Assistance with essays, recommendation letters, and submission timelines increases your success rate.

Funding Strategy: We help you combine scholarships with loan and savings plans for maximum financial efficiency.

Eligibility & Process

Eligibility Assessment

Our experts design a visa-compliant structure combining loans, show money, scholarships, and personal funds where applicable.

Approval & Confirmation

Visa-Ready Submission

You proceed with confidence, submitting verified financial proof that meets embassy and immigration requirements.
